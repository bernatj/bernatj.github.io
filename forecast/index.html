AI-forecast-based Attribution

Daily 6-day AI weather forecasts initialised from ERA5 reanalysis, run under both factual (current climate) and counterfactual (pre-industrial, PGW) conditions. The ACC signal = Factual − PGW, averaged over 13 initializations all verifying at the same target date (lead times 2–5 days). Stippled dots mark grid points not significant at p < 0.05 (paired t-test across initializations). Z500 factual contours are overlaid.

See Jiménez-Esteve et al. (2025) for methodology.
